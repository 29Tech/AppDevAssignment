The National Recycling Programme (NRP) in Singapore is a nationwide initiative aimed at promoting recycling and reducing waste. Managed by the National Environment Agency (NEA), the program provides households with dedicated recycling bins and regular collection services to encourage the separation and recycling of materials such as paper, plastic, glass, and metal. The NRP also includes public education campaigns to raise awareness about proper recycling practices and the importance of reducing contamination in recyclables. By making recycling convenient and accessible, the program supports Singapore's efforts to achieve its sustainability goals, including reducing waste sent to landfills and fostering a culture of environmental responsibility among residents.
