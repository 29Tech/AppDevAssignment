NEWater

Singapore Green Plan 2030 HDB Green Towns Programme National recycling programme NEWater OneMillionTrees movement Walk Cycle Ride SG Programme Agri-food cluster transformation (ACT fund) Zero Waster Masterplan National Hydrogen Strategy

NEWater is Singapore's innovative high-grade reclaimed water, produced through advanced purification processes that treat used water to meet stringent quality standards. Using a combination of microfiltration, reverse osmosis, and ultraviolet disinfection, NEWater is ultra-clean and safe for both non-potable and potable uses. It plays a crucial role in enhancing Singapore's water sustainability by reducing reliance on imported water and providing a reliable source of water for industrial and drinking purposes. NEWater is a cornerstone of Singapore's water management strategy, showcasing the country's commitment to innovation and self-sufficiency in water resources.
