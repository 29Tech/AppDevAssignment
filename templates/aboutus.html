Donki Mart

About Us!

Donki Mart is a leading retail chain specializing in Japanese products. From groceries to household items, we bring the best of Japan to your doorstep.

Our mission is to provide high-quality products at affordable prices, ensuring that our customers experience the true essence of Japanese culture.

Founded in 2020, Donki Mart has grown to become a household name, with stores across multiple countries and a strong online presence.

Our Sustainable Visionary...

At Donki Mart, we are committed to creating a greener future by integrating sustainable practices into every aspect of our business. Our Sustainability Roadmap (2025-2030) outlines a strategic path to reduce our environmental impact, support ethical sourcing, and engage our community in eco-friendly initiatives. We strive to purchase items that are made from recycled materials as a way to support ethical sourcing.

We begin with a strong foundation, focusing on energy efficiency, waste reduction, and partnerships with responsible suppliers. As we expand, we will introduce zero-waste initiatives, a dedicated line of sustainable products, and plastic-free operations. Our ultimate goal is to become an industry leader in sustainability, striving for net-zero carbon emissions, a circular economy model, and transparent environmental reporting.

By prioritizing innovation and responsibility, Donki Mart is shaping a retail experience that is not only convenient but also conscious of the planet. Together, with our customers and partners, we are building a more sustainable tomorrow.

The History of Donki Mart

Humble Beginnings (1978-1990s) Donki Mart was founded in 1978 with a simple yet ambitious goal: to provide quality products at affordable prices while delivering an exceptional shopping experience. Starting as a small neighborhood store, Donki Mart quickly gained a reputation for its wide selection, friendly service, and commitment to customer satisfaction. Throughout the 1980s and 1990s, the company steadily expanded, opening multiple locations and becoming a trusted name in retail.
