The Agri-food Cluster Transformation (ACT Fund) is a Singaporean initiative designed to support the transformation and modernization of the local agri-food industry, enhancing food security and sustainability. The fund provides financial assistance to farms and agri-food businesses to adopt advanced technologies, improve productivity, and implement sustainable practices such as energy-efficient systems and water recycling. By encouraging innovation and the use of high-tech solutions like vertical farming and automation, the ACT Fund aims to increase local food production, reduce reliance on imports, and build a resilient and future-ready agri-food sector in Singapore.
