Walk Cycle Ride SG Programme

The Walk Cycle Ride SG Programme is a Singaporean initiative aimed at promoting sustainable and active mobility by encouraging residents to walk, cycle, and use public transport instead of private vehicles. The program focuses on improving infrastructure, such as expanding cycling paths and pedestrian-friendly walkways, and enhancing connectivity between public transport nodes. It also includes public education campaigns to raise awareness about the benefits of active mobility for health, the environment, and reducing traffic congestion. By making walking, cycling, and public transport more convenient and accessible, the program supports Singapore's goals of reducing carbon emissions and creating a greener, more liveable city.
